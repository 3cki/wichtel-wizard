# Using Wichteln Wizard

A step-by-step guide to organizing your Secret Santa gift exchange.

## For Group Organizers

### Step 1: Create a Group

1. Go to the Wichteln Wizard homepage
2. Click "Create New Group"
3. Fill in the group details:
   - **Group Name**: e.g., "Smith Family Christmas 2024"
   - **Description** (optional): Add context like budget, theme, etc.
   - **Draw Date** (optional): Set when assignments will be made
4. Click "Create Group"
5. You'll receive a unique 6-character code (e.g., "ABC123")

### Step 2: Share the Code

Share your group code with participants through:
- Text message
- Email
- Social media
- Printed invitations

### Step 3: Monitor Participation

- Check who has joined your group
- See how many wishes each person has added
- Wait until everyone has joined and added their wishlists

### Step 4: Draw Secret Santa

Once everyone is ready:
1. Click the "Draw Secret Santa" button
2. Confirm the action (this cannot be undone!)
3. The system will:
   - Randomly assign each person to give a gift to someone else
   - Ensure no one gets themselves
   - Make assignments visible to all participants

## For Participants

### Step 1: Join a Group

1. Get the group code from your organizer
2. Go to Wichteln Wizard homepage
3. Enter the code in "Join Existing Group"
4. Click "Join Group"
5. You'll be assigned a fun anonymous name (e.g., "Jolly Reindeer")

### Step 2: Add Your Wishlist

1. Once joined, scroll to "My Wishlist"
2. Add gift ideas by filling in:
   - **Gift Idea**: Short title (e.g., "Board Game")
   - **Description**: More details (e.g., "Strategy games, not too complex")
   - **Product Link**: Optional URL to specific item
3. Add multiple wishes (3-5 is ideal)
4. You can delete wishes before the draw

### Step 3: Wait for the Draw

- Check back to see when Secret Santa has been drawn
- You'll be notified by the green "Secret Santa Drawn!" badge

### Step 4: See Your Assignment

After the draw:
1. Return to the group page
2. You'll see a green card showing:
   - Who you're buying for (their anonymous name)
   - Their complete wishlist with descriptions and links
3. Start shopping!

These names keep the exchange fun and anonymous!

## Tips for a Successful Wichteln

### For Organizers

- **Set a Budget**: Mention it in the group description
- **Set a Deadline**: Use the draw date to keep people on track
- **Remind Participants**: Send reminders to join and add wishes
- **Wait for Everyone**: Don't draw until all expected participants have joined

### For Participants

- **Be Specific**: Add detailed descriptions to help your Secret Santa
- **Include Links**: Direct product links make shopping easier
- **Add Multiple Options**: Give your Secret Santa choices
- **Set Price Ranges**: Be considerate of the agreed budget
- **Be Timely**: Join and add wishes promptly

## Privacy & Data

- Your email is optional and only used for notifications
- Your wishlist is only visible after the Secret Santa draw
- Only your assigned Secret Santa can see your wishes
- Group codes are unique and cannot be guessed
- Participant data is tied to your browser (localStorage)

## FAQ

**Q: Can I join multiple groups?**
A: Yes! Each group has its own code and operates independently.

**Q: Can I change my wishes after the draw?**
A: No, wishes are locked once Secret Santa is drawn.

**Q: What if someone doesn't add any wishes?**
A: They can still participate, but their Secret Santa won't have guidance.

**Q: Can I see who else is in the group?**
A: Yes, you can see all participants' anonymous names and wish counts.

**Q: Can the draw be undone?**
A: No, once drawn it's permanent to ensure fairness.

**Q: What if I lose my group code?**
A: Contact your group organizer. There's no way to retrieve it otherwise.

**Q: How long do groups last?**
A: Groups persist in the database. They remain accessible with the group code.
